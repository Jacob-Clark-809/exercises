DDL: Data Definition Language. This sub-language deals with creating the
schema for your database, such as creating tables and managaging contstraints.
Two DDL Statements are: CREATE TABLE and ALTER COLUMN.

DML: Data Manipulation Language. This sub-language deals with the data actually
contained within the tables of your database. In particular it deals with CRUD:
Create, Read, Update, Delete. Two DML statements are: SELECT and INSERT.
